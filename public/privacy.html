Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

At StyleBot, we collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, communicate with us, or use our styling services. This may include your email address, fashion preferences, and any images you upload for styling assistance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, particularly to personalize the fashion recommendations generated by our AI model. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Firebase. Image uploads are processed temporarily to provide outfit recommendations and are not used to train global AI models without your consent.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@stylebot.ai.
